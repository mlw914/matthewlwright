Get to know Matthew

Matthew L. Wright is a journalist, writer and aspiring host who works to provide and create spaces for marginalized voices to share their narratives.

​His interests includes social justice, politics and travel but his PASSIONS are entertainment and pop culture. He constantly scours social media for the latest in celebrity gossip and the going's of everyday life.

Matthew has a bachelor's degree in journalism and political science from Northwestern University's Medill School of Journalism (BSJ '16).

Matthew resides in New York City and uses his free time to learn Beyoncé's choreography, cook and travel.
